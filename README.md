ReLaunchAI
AI-Powered Workforce Re-entry Platform for Women in Tech
Problem Statement
Many women who take career breaks and wish to return to technology careers face difficulty due to rapidly evolving tools, cloud platforms, AI advancements, and modern development practices. Enterprises struggle to systematically reintegrate this experienced yet underutilized talent pool.
Our Solution
ReLaunchAI is a B2B AI-powered platform that helps enterprises assess, upskill, and reintegrate women returning to diverse technology roles such as software development, cloud engineering, data science, AI/ML, and DevOps.
